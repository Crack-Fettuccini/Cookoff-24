CodeRush: 24-Hour Hackathon

Feb 1st - Feb 2nd, 2022 | CodeChef Vit Chennai

Day 1

  • Event Kickoff (Morning)
  • Hackathon Coding Phase 1
  • Tech Talk or Workshop (Late Afternoon)

Day 2

  • Hackathon Coding Phase 2
  • Sponsor Speech (Late Afternoon)
  • Closing Ceremony and Awards (Night)

Rules

  • The coding round is a team event, and each team consists of 3 members from the same batch.
  • To keep the competition fair, teams are categorized by year (1st, 2nd, or 3rd).
  • Each team will receive a set of questions based on different themes, with difficulty levels tailored to their respective batch.
